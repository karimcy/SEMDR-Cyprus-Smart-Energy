Hospitality Focus

The SEMDR project aims to revolutionize energy use in Cyprus's hotels by introducing smart energy management and demand response capabilities.

30+ Hotels Targeted

Renewable Integration

Our core objective is to help integrate renewable energy sources, reduce energy costs for hotels without compromising guest comfort, and enhance the stability of the Cypriot electricity grid.

25% Energy Savings

Advanced Technology

This project advances our innovative solution through comprehensive testing and validation, moving from demonstration to a fully operational system within a partner hotel.
